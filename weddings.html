Weddings

Your Love Story Deserves to Be Told Beautifully

Every love story is unique. As a wedding officiant, Meghan brings the same authenticity, warmth, and heartfelt storytelling she’s known for on stage to the most meaningful day of your life. Whether you envision a ceremony that is simple and intimate or elaborate and grand, Meghan is honored to help you create a celebration that reflects your values, your journey, and your love.

💍 Ceremonies with Heart

Meghan offers officiant services for:

  • Traditional weddings
  • Non-traditional & inclusive weddings
  • Elopements
  • Vow renewals
  • Commitment ceremonies

Each ceremony is custom-written to honor your personal love story, your shared dreams, and your vision for the future.

🌟 A Professional with Heart

Meghan’s background in public speaking, recovery, and personal growth makes her uniquely skilled at delivering heartfelt, memorable ceremonies that resonate. Her style is grounded, uplifting, and authentic, ensuring your moment is one you and your guests will cherish.
